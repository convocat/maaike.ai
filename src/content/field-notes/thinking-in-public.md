There's value in sharing ideas before they're fully formed. It invites collaboration, surfaces blind spots, and creates accountability to develop thoughts further.

The [[digital-gardens-vs-blogs|digital garden]] model encourages this by making the maturity of each piece visible. Readers know when something is a draft versus a finished argument.

This is the opposite of the traditional academic model, where ideas are only shared after extensive peer review.
