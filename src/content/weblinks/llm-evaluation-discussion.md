An interesting discussion on how to evaluate the quality of LLM outputs. The main tension is between automated metrics (which are fast but imprecise) and human evaluation (which is slow but more meaningful).

Key takeaway: the best evaluation approach depends on what you're optimising for. If it's factual accuracy, automated metrics work. If it's nuance and reasoning quality, you need humans in the loop.
