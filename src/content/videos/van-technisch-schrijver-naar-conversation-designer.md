The shift is not about learning entirely new skills but about applying existing expertise in a new context, leveraging experience with documentation, user guides, and information architecture while expanding into dialogue design, tone and voice development, and interaction patterns. The future of technical communication is conversational, and the intersection of technical writing and conversation design represents how organizations will interact with their users in an increasingly digital world."
