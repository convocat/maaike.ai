## Great exchanges

### Brain dump suggestion (March 2026)
**Context:** Maaike had a load of ideas and didn't want to lose them.
**What worked:** Rather than jumping to a solution, Claude asked "what does the inside of your head feel like — scattered or connected?" and proposed brain dump first, split into seeds later.
**Why it worked:** Met her where she was. No friction. Deferred structure until it was useful.
